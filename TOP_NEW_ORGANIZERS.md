## Reading this

The campaign's new organizers are overwhelmingly **student/university hackathon hosts** (via Unstop) —
directly the Tier 1 university ecosystem the phase prioritised, reached not by crawling campus websites
(which publish no machine-readable feeds) but by the platform where those organizers actually publish
their events. This is the honest, measured shape of India's upcoming tech-event organizer graph as it
is machine-readable today.
